About out project:

Our aim was to create a simple educational site site with three pages, with the main page being an illustrated map of a typical scene in Monterey Bay in which users could click on an environment or animal residing in it to learn more fact about that animal/environment. Our secondary page is a live page which uses an API to keep track of bird sightings in the area. Our target audience is anybody who wants to learn more about the environment in Monterey Bay. This is why our descriptions are kept simple and easy to understand, so that anyone can learn simple facts, regardless of where they may reside or their age/previous knowledge of our environment
